FAQ
  • How do I create an account?

    To create an account, simply click on the "Create Account" button on the homepage and enter your email address and a password. You will then be able to log in to your account and start using our services.

  • How does it benefit tenants?

    Tenants can easily search for available properties, submit maintenance requests, and make rent payments online. It improves transparency and communication with property owners.

  • How do I handle maintenance requests through the system?

    Tenants can submit maintenance requests online, and property managers can assign tasks to maintenance personnel, track progress, and communicate updates.

  • What happens in case of late rent payments?

    The system can automate late fee calculations and reminders for tenants, helping property owners enforce rent payment policies.

  • What is tenant screening, and why is it important?

    Tenant screening is the process of evaluating potential tenants to determine their suitability for a rental property. It involves background checks, credit checks, and rental history verification to ensure that tenants are reliable and trustworthy.
